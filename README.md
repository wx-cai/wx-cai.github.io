I am currently a PhD student in Stanford University's Department of Electrical Engineering. I am advised by [Prof. Thomas H. Lee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_H._Lee_(electrical_engineer)) and work closely with [Prof. Ali Keshavarzi](https://profiles.stanford.edu/ali-keshavarzi).

**I work on the theory and applications of low-power computing with analog circuits.** I also have a background in multi-modal LLMs and computer vision.

I received my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University in 2026 and my B.S. in Automation from Southeast University in 2024. In 2025, I received the Samsung Semiconductor Scholarship Award.
